About the Book
Articles and reviews from your favorite writers. The low-down on what’s fresh in tech, comics, movies, and music. Wrapped in a style all its own. Named after the word ‘flippersmack’ which is what a penguin does when it slaps another object. The penguin usually jumps one to two feet off the ground when it does this.
Your very own taste of insanity to inspire, inform, and entertain. From the creators of System Failure and Avalanche, we present to you - FLIPPERSMACK!
